What We Offer

Our Services

Planning & Reporting

Prepare project schedule and budget baselines; review and approve contractor plans; monitor progress; prepare monthly owner reports; raise early warning flags.

Contracts Management

Coordinate and prepare contracts for contractors, vendors, and consultants; add and modify clauses to limit variations; manage execution through to closing.

Procurement Management

Recommend contractor strategy; qualify suitable parties; prepare RFPs, evaluate bids; negotiate commercially with contractors, vendors, and consultants.

Cost Control

Manage the dedicated project bank account; track expenditures, variations, and change orders throughout the entire project lifecycle.

Design Management

Administrate timelines and deliverables of the design stage; monitor design progress; coordinate closely with project designers for best constructability outcomes.

Quality Management

Establish quality management methodology; supervise work quality; perform regular site inspections; issue NCRs; review and approve contractor invoices.

Value Engineering

Review design packages to create value and suggest alternatives; engage with designers in technical discussions for best constructability solutions.

Document Control

Establish a project documentation management platform (e.g. Aconex); maintain clear project archives; maintain technical and commercial logs and registers.
